# Governance

Running meetings, managing finances, strategic planning, volunteers, grants, and everything else a committee handles. Sourced from ClubIQ (Sport and Recreation QLD) and the Oxford Croquet library.

Sport and Recreation QLD's comprehensive club management resource. Ten guides covering every governance area:

| Guide | What it covers |

| [[resources/clubiq/club_hub_financial_management\|Financial Management]] | Budgeting, financial basics, reporting |
| [[resources/clubiq/club_hub_grants and other revenue\|Grants & Revenue]] | Finding and applying for grants, alternative revenue |
| [[resources/clubiq/club_hub_facilities_grounds\|Facilities & Grounds]] | Managing club facilities and grounds |
| [[resources/clubiq/club_hub_event_management\|Event Management]] | Planning and running club events |
| [[resources/clubiq/club_hub_volunteers_and_helpers\|Volunteers & Helpers]] | Recruiting, retaining, and managing volunteers |
| [[resources/clubiq/club_hub_safety_risk_management\|Safety & Risk]] | Risk assessment, safety management |
| [[resources/clubiq/club_hub_member_growth_marketing\|Member Growth & Marketing]] | Growing membership, marketing the club |
| [[resources/clubiq/club_hub_running_your_club_planning_for_the_future\|Planning for the Future]] | Strategic planning, succession, long-term thinking |
