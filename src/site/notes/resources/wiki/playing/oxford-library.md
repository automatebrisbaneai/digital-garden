# Oxford Croquet Library

The Oxford Croquet library (`resources/oxford-croquet/`) is one of the most comprehensive croquet knowledge resources in English. Eight sub-collections covering coaching, laws, equipment, lawn care, club management, promotions, health and safety, and tournament management.

All articles are written for UK conditions — temperate climate, cool-season grasses, UK regulations. Apply judgement when using for QLD: the croquet technique is universal, but lawn care and legal references need local adaptation.

## How to use it

The Oxford articles are written as individual standalone pieces. Browse by sub-collection or search within the folder. There is no master index — the sub-collection folder structure is the navigation.

For lawn care specifically: always cross-reference with the [[resources/lawn-care/index\|QLD Lawn Care collection]] first. The Oxford articles tell you the principles; the QLD collection tells you what actually applies here.
