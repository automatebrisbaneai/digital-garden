# Come & Try

A structured program that gives curious people a proper front door into croquet — and gives clubs a reliable way to find new members.

Someone visits [comeandtrycroquet.com](https://comeandtrycroquet.com), puts their hand up, and gets matched to their nearest club. A volunteer books a taste test. A four-week email sequence runs in the background. Three visits later, many of them join.
