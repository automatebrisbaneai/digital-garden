# Playing Croquet

Tactics, rules, and strategy for Golf Croquet and Association Croquet — beginner to advanced.

---

## Which game are you playing?

**Golf Croquet (GC)** is the simpler, faster game. One ball per turn. First to run six hoops wins. Most Queensland social play and Come & Try sessions use Golf Croquet.

**Association Croquet (AC)** is the deeper strategic game. Multi-ball turns, breaks, leaves. Rewards long-term planning and precise striking.
